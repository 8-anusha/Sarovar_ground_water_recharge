FAQ'S

Whats our mission?
Our project will identify and establish water recharge points in a ward-wise manner, ensuring efficient and sustainable groundwater recharge.
How can Citizens work with the Government?
Citizen groups can help achieve this convergence between institutions.Engagement varies from advocacy, awareness building and citizen oversight, for example, groups being authorised by BBMP to maintain lakes themselves.
How does the Government see Citizen Participation?
Every agency has their own way “institutionalising citizen participation”. For example, BBMP has entered into partnerships with lake groups through formal MoUs. KSPCB has created Watchdog Committees for water quality, comprising of government agencies and citizens And the Karnataka Lake Conservation and Development Authority has created the post of the Lake Warden.
How does public interact with this website?
Users can enter their pincode and alert the areas flooding around them. Based on the number of alerts received , sarovar will convey the same to the respective authorities
